What is LivingHistory Images?

LivingHistory Images uses WebGPU and FamilySearch to show you image memories attached to a deceased ancestor from FamilySearch.

What is LivingHistory AI Chat?

LivingHistory AI Chat uses FamilySearch data on your deceased ancestors. An AI character is generated for you to interact with based on a deceased ancestor's biographical data from FamilySearch.

How do I login to FamilySearch?

You will be presented with a button to Login to FamilySearch. A new tab will be opened for you to login to FamilySearch.

How do I select a deceased ancestor?

After logging into FamilySearch, you will be given a drop down list of 4 generations of your deceased ancestors to select from.
